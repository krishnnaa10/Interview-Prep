Q. What are Operators in SQL ?


- Operators are symbols used to perform operations on data. They allow you to manipulate and evaluate 
  expressions within your SQL queries.

  Types of Operators:

    Arithmetic Operators: +, -, *, /, %
    Comparison Operators: =, <, >, !=, <=, >=
    Logical Operators: AND, OR, NOT
    Bitwise Operators: &, |, ^
