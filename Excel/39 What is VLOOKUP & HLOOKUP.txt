Q. What is VLOOKUP & HLOOKUP ?


- VLOOKUP & HLOOKUP is a powerful function in Excel that is used to retrieve data from a table
  based on the LOOKUP value.

  Formula -

  VLOOKUP : (LOOKUP Value, Table Array, Column Index No., [range lookup])
  HLOOKUP : (LOOKUP Value, Table Array, Row Index No., [range lookup])
