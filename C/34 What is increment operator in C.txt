Q. What is increment operator in C ?


- The increment ( ++ ) operator in C are used for incrementing the numeric values by
  1 respectively.

  Types- 
  
  pre-increment : the increment operator is used as the prefix. Also known as prefix increment. 
  ie.. ++num;

  post-increment : the increment operator is used as the suffix. Also known as postfix increment. 
  ie.. num++;
