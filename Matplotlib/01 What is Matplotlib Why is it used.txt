Q. What is Matplotlib? Why is it used?


- Matplotlib is a Python library for creating static, animated, and interactive visualizations. It 
  was introduced by John Hunter in year 2002.

  It is primarily used for:

  -> Plotting 2D and 3D graphs
  -> Customizing graphs with labels, legends, and colors
  -> Saving visualizations in multiple formats (PNG, PDF, SVG, etc.)
