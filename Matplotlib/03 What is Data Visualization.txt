Q. What is Data Visualization?


- Data visualization is the graphical representation of information and data. It uses visual elements
  like charts, graphs, and maps to make complex data more understandable and accessible. 

         By turning raw data into visual stories, data visualization helps people to identify patterns,
  trends, and outliers that might be difficult to spot in a spreadsheet or other text-based format.

  Key benefits of Data Visualization :

  -> Improved understanding
  -> Enhanced decision-making
  -> Effective communication
  -> Storytelling
